Graded Problems

P10. Write a program that prints out every fourth number from 1 to 100 (that is, 4,8,12,...,100). Use a loop.

P11. Modify the Grader program from p 99 to print out pluses and minuses for grades. For example, A- for 91 and C+ for 78. If the grade is greater than 60 and ends in 8 or 9, it gets a plus. If the grade is greater than 60 and ends in 0, 1, or 2, it gets a minus.
