Graded Problems

P14. Write a program that asks the user for information about an employee, including their salary. The program will then print the employee's salary for 5 years, after asking the user for the percent raise to give.

Here's a sample run:
Please enter your name: Bill Clinton
Please enter your address: 1600 Pennsylvania Ave.
Please enter your phone number: 5555555
Please enter your salary: 200000

What percent raise do you expect? 5

Salary for Bill Clinton:
Year 1: $200000
Year 2: $210000
Year 3: $220500
Year 4: $231525
Year 3: $243101.25
(The words in bold were entered by the user.)

(Hint: Use a loop, and don't worry about formatting the salary (that is, how many places after the decimal are displayed) since we haven't talked about how to do that yet.)
