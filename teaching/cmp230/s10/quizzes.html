General Information

A substantial part of the grade for this course is based on on-line quizzes.

Topics: The material for the quizzes comes primarily from the assigned reading from the textbooks. Each quiz covers a chapter or topic and can be completed in twenty minutes.

Due Dates: The target dates for quizzes are located on the front page for the class. These are the suggested day by which you complete the quiz. However, you may take the quiz as many times as you like, up until the deadline for each quiz. The deadline for most quizzes is three weeks after the target date. See the home page for this class for target dates and deadlines of individual quizzes. Any quizzes you do not complete by the deadline will be assigned a grade of 0.

Blackboard

This course will use the on-line Blackboard system for submitting work electronically and on-line quizzes. Blackboard should be accessible through your CUNY portal account (http://portal.cuny.edu). If you have not used Blackboard in the past, information is available at the Computer Center.  They can set up your account and have information sheets about using the system.

Timing Out: If the system times out and locks your quiz attempt (happens rarely when the browser or PC crashes), send me mail and I can clear the attempt so you can try again.
