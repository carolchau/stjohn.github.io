Challenge Problem 1

CMP 230: Programming Methods I
Lehman College, City University of New York
Spring 2010

With two other people, work out the following problem:

Create an Alice world with a jumping animal (e.g. a frog, a bunny, an insect) and have the animal repeatedly jump a random amount into the air and then back to the ground.
  • The animal should stop his jumping after he has a jump of over 20 meters.
  • You should display, using 3DText the height of the current jump and the highest jump thus far.
At the end of the program, display the height of the highest jump.
