General Information About the Lab

For information about getting started, see Lab 1.

In today's lab, we focus on handling multiple objects via arrays in Alice.

Bug Zapper

Create a world with a list or an array that contains bugs from the Bugs gallery (in Animals). When the world is played, the bugs should all move quickly in different random directions. When the user successfully clicks a bug, it vanishes. The game should continue until all of the bugs are gone.

Soccer

Create a world with a soccer goal and 5 soccer balls (see the Sports gallery). When the user click the mouse on a ball, move it towards the goal, and then make the ball vanish. Continue the game until all of the balls are gone.

Race Cars

Create a world where 5 cars are racing around a track forever. Every lap, randomly choose a car to move forward an extra 3 meters in front of the rest, to simulate different cars overtaking one another in a race.

Take a Bow

Extend your rockette world from Lab 9 so that one of the dancers, at random, takes a bow at the end of the performance. (Hint: make an array and randomly choose the index of the dancer to taka a bow).
