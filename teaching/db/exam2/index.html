  • All books and bags must be left at the front of the classroom during this exam. This is to keep the aisles clear, so that I can easily reach people to answer questions.
  • You must be enrolled in the course to take the exam.
  • When you enter the classroom, leave your books and bads at the front of the room and find your exam (they are in alphabetic order from the front of the room). This is so everyone has the same amount of time to complete the exam.
