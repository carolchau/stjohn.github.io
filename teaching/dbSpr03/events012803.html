  • Summer Research Opportunities:  
    The National Science Foundation funds Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REUs) in all the sciences, engineering, and mathematics.  The deadlines are fast approaching:  
    • A list of all sites for next summer
    • The Polytechnical University of New York program (brochure available early February)
    • The Rutgers University Program (located in New Jersey and includes a 2 week research trip to Europe).  This program has both computer science and mathematics projects, and the deadline is 31 January!

    • AAUW Fellowship Program for women pursuing M.A. in computer science (10 January)
    • AT&T Labs Fellowship Program (15 January)
    (Deadlines have past for this year, but worth considering for next year:
    • Ford Foundation Predoctoral Fellowships (20 November)
    • GEM Fellowship Program (1 December)
    • National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowships (7 November))
