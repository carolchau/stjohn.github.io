NY Fed Data

The Federal Reserve Bank of New York provides economical and financial data about the greater New York Region. We will use the following data set:
  • Labor Market for Recent College Graduates: Scroll to the bottom of the page, and click on the "Download Data." The file will be .xlsx, and the data is on the last sheet. Open in your favorite spreadsheet program (for example, Google Drive or Microsoft Excel). Before exporting, double check to see if there are commas in the salary numbers (if there are, highlight those cells, click on Format > Cells, and then uncheck the box for displaying thousands separators). Export/download as a CSV file.
