Homework 1

Cluster V: Computers and Robots
COSMOS Program
UC Davis
Summer 2001

This homework is to be completed during the Monday evening session, July 9, in Physics 106.
  1. Log on to a computer in the lab, using the account given to you this morning.
  2. Launch the Netscape browser (this can be done from the icon bar or by typing netscape in a terminal window).
  3. Bring up the course webpage: http://comet.lehman.cuny.edu/stjohn/teaching/legos and print out the information for the trip to the Tech museum.
  4. Bring up the Tech Museum's webpage: http://www.techmuseum.org.
  5. While at the Tech Museum, you must visit attend a special program, in addition to the "Meet Laika and Chip, the Robot Dogs" and "Peanut Butter and Jelly Robot" programs. Choose one of the daily programs and tours that looks interesting to you, and fill it in, along with its location, on the information sheet you printed.
  6. While at the Tech website, look up the Robot Zoo (under the Online Exhibits). Choose one of the animals (under the "Who's in the Zoo?" list on the right side of the page). A Lego Mindstorms robot comes with 2 motors, 2 touch sensors, and a light sensor. Examine the schematics of your animal.
    • How many motors does the robot use?

    • How many touch sensors?

    • How many light sensors?

    • Does it use additional sensors that are not found in the Lego Mindstorms kit?

  7. Also on the Online Exhibits page is a list of Revolutionaries. Choose one of them, read the interview, and then print and complete the puzzle. (Note: most, but not all, have puzzles. Choose one that has a puzzle listed in its icon bar).
