Information about First Examination in Computer Science
June 2002

General Information

  • The examination is Tuesday, 11 June 2002.
  • This page contains information about the algorithms section of the exam. For questions about the other sections, contact the instructors of those core courses during the 2001-2002 academic year.
  • "Cheat sheets" will not be allowed in the exam.
  • The exam was written by a departmental committe that contains the instructors from the past two years. Looking at their websites and exams will give an overview of topics on the exam.
  • Get the packet of exams from the departmental office and do the problems from the old exams.
  • Work through all past exams, including the final from last year.
