  • Answer the questions below about the special programs and the Innovation and Communication Galleries:
    1. (Robot dogs): How many different sensors does each dog have?

    2. (Robot dogs): Sensors are an example of "input" devices, and motors are an example of "output" devices. What other output devices do the robot dogs have?

    3. (PB&J): What was the most difficult step of making a sandwich to describe to the robot?

    4. (PB&J): An algorithm is a step-by-step set of instructions to accomplish a task. Does the algorithm for making a peanut butter and jelly sandwich change if you give the directions in Spanish instead of English? Why or why not?

    5. (Innovation Gallery): Explore the Bike Design Studio. What material would you select? Why?

    6. (Innovation Gallery): Have a "map" made of your head. How is this different from a regular photograph?

    7. (Communication Gallery): How is information transmitted on a fiber optics network? What is the "fiber" made of?

    8. (Communication Gallery): Besides fiber optics networks, how else can information be transmitted?
