Project Ideas

LSP 150: Computers & Society
Fall 2004
Lehman College-- CUNY


For the project for this course, you have the choice of writing a 10-15 page term paper, expanding on the topics covered in class, or building a robot and designing the accompanying programs.  

If you choose to build a robot:

  • Your project can have up to 5 programs (the total size of all loaded programs cannot exceed 6K).
  • Different lego configurations for different programs are possible, but you must be able to convert from one to another quickly.
  • Design ideas need to be submitted by 1 December, to make sure that they're the right level of difficulty for the project.
Here's some possible ideas:
  • a jukebox that plays songs on request
  • a robot with an arm (see the last chapter of Baum's book)
  • a robot that navigates a maze using the light sensor and bumpers
  • a team of robots that cooperates in completing a task (like playing tag, relay race, moving an object, etc.)
  • an aerial tram
  • some projects from the course at Brown University: http://www.cs.brown.edu/courses/cs148/old/2004/final/index.html
  • a clock
  • a plotter
