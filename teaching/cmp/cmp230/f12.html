Announcements:

  • Please bring textbook and storage device (ie USB keydrive or floppy) to every class.
  • You will need to use your Blackboard account to turn in assignments. If you are unfamiliar with Blackboard or need help accessing your account, there is extra help available during the first two weeks of classes at the Computer Center.
  • Programming problem sets are due most weeks at midnight on Friday. See outline below for deadlines.
  • Programs and notes can be found at the book's site. We are using the second edition (Python 3) of Zelle's Python Programming: An Introduction to Computer Science. See the syllabus below for more details.
  • You should install python on your home machine. It is free from www.python.org.
