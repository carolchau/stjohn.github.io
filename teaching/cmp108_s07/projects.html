Project Information

CMP 108: Programming for Non-Majors
Spring 2007
Lehman College-- CUNY


For the project for this course, you have the choice of building a robot and designing the accompanying programs, or designing an animation and writing the accompanying programs.

Design ideas need to be submitted by 24 April, to make sure that they're the right level of difficulty for the project.

If you choose to build a robot:
  • Your project can have up to 5 programs (the total size of all loaded programs cannot exceed 6K).
  • Different lego configurations for different programs are possible, but you must be able to convert from one to another quickly.
  • In your design ideas and abstract, list the parts (sensors and motors) that you plan to use.
If you choose to design an animation:
  • Your project can have up to 5 different scene changes in your virtual world.
  • There are hundreds of models in the standard galleries, but if you may design your own for the animation.  Indicate any models you plan in your design ideas and abstract.
