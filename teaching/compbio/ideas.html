CMP 464/788: Introduction to Computational Biology
Lehman College, City University of New York
Fall 2003


Sequence Alignment

  • Present the analysis of running times (ie proofs) for the standard dynamic programming algorithms for pairwise alignment (Needleman-Wunsch, Smith-Waterman)
  • Present the concept of Hidden Markov Models (HMMs) and how they are used in sequence alignment.  Compare and constrast various methods that use HMMs.
  • How are FASTA and BLAST different?  Explain and compare the strengths and limitations of these two approaches.
