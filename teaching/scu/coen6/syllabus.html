  • Grading: The grading for the course will be based on:
    • Laboratory: 10%
    • Homework: 15%
    • Midterm Exam 1: 20%
    • Midterm Exam 2: 25%
    • Final Exam: 30%
  • Exams: The exams will be
    • Midterm Exam 1: Monday, 26 January in class.
    • Midterm Exam 2: During 8th week of class, TBA.
    • Final Exam: Friday, 20 March, 9:10-12:10.
    You must pass the final to pass the class. Since the final is comprehensive, if you do better on it than on one of the midterm exams, the score on the final may replace one of the midterm scores. Note that the final will be long and difficult, so, it is an exceptionally bad idea to plan on taking advantage of this.
  • Assignments: Lab assignments are due at the end of each laboratory period, unless specifically told otherwise. Homework assignments are due Thursdays at noon. Late assignments will have 25 percent of their score deducted for each day late.
  • Attendance: Attendance in lecture and laboratory is mandatory.
  • Honor Code: You are encouraged to work together on the overall design of the programs and homework. However, for specific programs and homework assignments, all work must be your own. See Undergraduate Handbook for more information about the School of Engineering Honor Code.
