Research Page of Katherine St. John

My research interests lie in random structures and algorithms, languages, logic, and database theory. Most recently, I have been working the expressibility of formal languanges over random structures, such as bit strings and trees.

I am co-organizing a minisymposium at the annual meeting of SIAM on random structures and logic. Here's our session if you're going to be in Toronto in July.
