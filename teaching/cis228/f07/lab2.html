Lounge, Revisited

Before starting the lounge exercises, you will need to download the files provided on the book's webpage to a floppy drive or USB keychain.  The book's website is:
http://headfirstlabs.com/books/hfhtml/index.php
The goal is to create organize the lounge website, using folders.  We'll follow the book's directions.  Here's an outline:
  1. Organize the lounge site into folders (see p 60), grouping like items together.
  2. Fix the links on the pages to include relative paths.  Don't forget to also fix image links and links to parent directories (ie in directions.html).
  3. Do the Relativity Grand Challenge on p 70.

All-About-Me Website

The final activity for today's lab is to build a website with information about you.  We'll start with the page you built last class and add more. 
  • If you didn't do so last class, make a folder, called allAboutMe (the root folder) to hold your website and put your webpage from last time in the folder, renamed as index.html (why should you name it index.html?)
  • Create a folder inside allAboutMe to hold images. Choose two images (ie your favorite drinks from the book's files) and store them in that directory.
  • Make a new folder to contain a page about your favorite foods. This page should have at least two links to images (ie your favorite drinks, now residing in your images folder).
  • Link your pages together: create a link from your allAboutMe page to your favorite foods page, and vice versa.
