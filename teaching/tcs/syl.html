  • Textbook: Introduction to the Theory of Computation by M. Sipser, Thompson Publishing, 1997 (ISBN 0-534-94728-X).
  • Grading: The grading for the course will be based on:
    • Homework: 15%
    • Midterm Exams: 25%
    • Final Exam: 35%
  • Exams: The exams will be
    • Midterm Exam I: Friday, 4 October.
    • Midterm Exam II: Friday, 22 November.
    • Final: Friday, 20 December.
    Since the final is comprehensive, if you do better on it than on the midterm exam, the score on the final may replace one or both of the midterm exam scores. Note that the final will be long and difficult, so, it is an exceptionally bad idea to plan on taking advantage of this. There will be no makeup exams, and you must pass the final to pass the class.
  • Assignments: The homework assignments are available at the class website. No late homework is accepted. The lowest homework score will be not be counted when calculating the homework grade.
  • Honor Code: You are encouraged to work together on the overall design of the programs and homework. However, for specific homework assignments, all work must be your own.
