Thursday Afternoon: Making Decisions

Overview

To make our plots of earthquakes more informative, we are going to give earthquakes of different magnitudes different colors:

  • white: usually not felt or by a few at rest (< 2.5 in magnitude)
  • yellow: felt, but minimal damage (bigger than 2.5 but less than 6)
  • red: major earthquake that causes much damage (6 or higher).

To do this, we will need to make decisions, based on the magnitude. Python provides a useful construct to make decisions, called if statements.
