"Do Now" Worksheets

Below is a set of introductory ("do now") worksheets for a possible activity when students first enter the classroom. The worksheets are designed to be printed and worked out with pencil by pairs of students. Each should take a five to ten minutes while computers are being passed out and set up:

  • Do Now #1: Paper turtles (graph paper computing with paper turtles, named match to the lessons)
  • Do Now #2: Where in the World?
  • Do Now #3: Give USGS CSV file sample. Have students circle values when formatted nicely. Have them find it when just separated by commas.
  • Do Now #4: Pseudocode of loops (more graph paper computing)
  • Do Now #5: Nathan's triangulating to find earthquakes from the middle school ornithology curriculum...
